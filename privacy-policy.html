DarkPlayer Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 29, 2026

DarkPlayer is a local-first audio and video player focused on equalizer playback, local importing, WiFi Transfer, Google Drive importing, and background playback controls. This policy explains what information is handled when you use the app.

Summary

  • DarkPlayer stores imported media files inside the app's local sandbox on your device.
  • DarkPlayer does not sell personal data.
  • DarkPlayer does not operate its own user account system.
  • Google Sign-In is used only when you choose to import files from Google Drive.
  • WiFi Transfer is available only while you open the transfer screen in the app.

Information DarkPlayer Handles

Local media files

When you import audio or video from Files, Photos, WiFi Transfer, or Google Drive, DarkPlayer saves a copy in the app's local storage so you can play it offline. The app may read basic media metadata such as title, artist, duration, file type, and artwork or thumbnails in order to display your library and player controls.

Photos and Files access

If you choose to import from Photos or Files, iOS may ask for permission. DarkPlayer uses the selected items only for importing media or choosing artwork. The app does not scan your entire photo library unless you explicitly select items.

WiFi Transfer

WiFi Transfer starts a temporary local upload page on your device. Devices on the same WiFi network can open the shown address or QR code and upload audio or video files. Uploaded files are saved into DarkPlayer's local app storage. The upload page stops when you stop transfer or close the transfer flow.

Google Drive import

If you choose Google Drive import, DarkPlayer uses Google Sign-In to request access to list and download audio or video files you select from your Google Drive. Google handles the sign-in process. DarkPlayer does not receive your Google password. Access tokens are used for the import flow and are governed by Google's policies.

Background playback and lock screen controls

DarkPlayer may provide the current track title, artist, artwork, duration, and playback position to iOS media controls so playback can be controlled from the lock screen, headphones, Bluetooth devices, or car systems.

Data Sharing

DarkPlayer does not sell your personal data. Media imported into DarkPlayer is stored locally on your device unless you use iOS sharing, backups, or other system features outside the app. Google Drive sign-in and file access are provided by Google and subject to Google's privacy practices.

Data Retention and Deletion

Imported files remain in DarkPlayer until you delete them in the app or remove the app from your device. Deleting the app may remove locally stored app data, depending on iOS backup and restore settings.

Children's Privacy

DarkPlayer is not directed to children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when app features or legal requirements change. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the support contact listed on DarkPlayer's App Store product page.
